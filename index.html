Miles Morales is a Brooklyn-raised teenager of African-American and Puerto Rican descent who becomes a Spider-Man after being bitten by a genetically altered spider. Mentored by Peter Parker, he possesses unique abilities like camouflage and "venom blasts" (bio-electricity). He is a, witty, artistic, and courageous hero navigating his identity.

The mask has big white eye lenses outlined in red, and in Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse the animators slightly change
the eye shape to show his emotions — even though it’s a full mask.
The red spider logo looks like it was spray-painted. That’s because Miles loves graffiti, and the design reflects his artistic personality.

Timeline Timeline Timeline

  • Growing Up in Brooklyn

    Miles Morales was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York.
    He is the son of Jefferson Davis, a police officer, and Rio Morales, a nurse.
    Miles is smart, creative, and loves music and art. He feels pressure to succeed, especially after getting into a prestigious school. Even before becoming Spider-Man, Miles struggled with responsibility and identity.

  • The Spider Bite

    Miles’ life changes when he is bitten by a genetically modified spider.
    At first, he is confused and scared. He does not understand his new powers.
    Soon, he realizes he can:

    Stick to walls

    Turn invisible

    Use bio-electric “Venom” blasts

    Unlike Peter Parker, Miles has unique abilities that make him different from other Spider-Men

  • Meeting Peter Parker

    After gaining his powers, Miles meets Peter Parker.
    Peter becomes his mentor and tries to teach him what it truly means to be Spider-Man.

    Peter tells him an important lesson:

    “With great power comes great responsibility.”

    Miles looks up to Peter but also feels the weight of living up to his legacy.

    When Peter dies (in some versions of the story), Miles feels guilty and unsure of himself. But this tragedy pushes him to step up.

  • Becoming Spider-Man

    At first, Miles doubts himself.
    He feels he is not ready. He thinks he is not “the real Spider-Man.”

    But eventually, he understands something powerful:

    Anyone can wear the mask.

    Miles creates his own black-and-red Spider-Man suit.
    He chooses to fight for his city and protect the people of New York.

    He stops trying to be like Peter and starts becoming his own hero.

  • A Hero in His Own Way

    Miles becomes a symbol of courage, diversity, and hope.
    He balances school, family, and being a superhero.

    He proves that being Spider-Man is not about being perfect
    it’s about making the right choice when it matters.

    Miles Morales is not just another Spider-Man.
    He is his own hero.

Venom Blast

Miles can generate bio-electric energy called Venom power.

Camouflage

Miles can turn invisible for a short time.

Spider-Sense

He has a danger-detecting ability that warns him about threats.
It reacts before something bad happens.

Wall-Crawling and Web-Shoting

Miles can stick to walls and ceilings just like a spider. Also, Like other Spider-Men, Miles uses web-shooters to swing through the city and shoot webs.

Enemies of Miles Enemies of Miles Enemies of Miles

The Prowler

The Prowler is a dangerous and skilled fighter.
In some versions of the story, he is Aaron Davis — Miles’ uncle.
He uses advanced technology and stealth tactics.
His connection to Miles makes their conflict emotional and personal.

The Tinkerer

The Tinkerer is a genius engineer who creates powerful weapons and tech.
In Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales, she leads the Underground.
She believes she is fighting for justice but becomes one of Miles’ biggest threats.

Rhino

Rhino is a super-strong villain wearing an armored suit.
He relies on brute force and destruction.
Miles must use agility and strategy to defeat him.

Kingpin

Kingpin is a powerful crime boss in New York.
He controls criminal networks and manipulates events from the shadows.
Even without superpowers, he is extremely dangerous.

The Spot

The Spot can create portals through space.
He can disappear and attack from unexpected places.
His unpredictable powers make him one of the most unique enemies Miles faces.
